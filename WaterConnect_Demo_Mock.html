What to point out while demoing

Grounded answers. Every reply cites the ministry's own manuals and national standards — the government owns the knowledge base.

Humans in the loop. Uncertain cases escalate to the district engineer (scenario 3). The assistant augments local technicians, never replaces them.

A sensor for the sector. Every exchange is logged — query patterns show the ministry where breakdowns cluster and which skills gaps persist.

Works on any phone. WhatsApp today; USSD, SMS and voice channels reuse the same architecture proven by Akvo's AgriConnect in Kenya.

Rural and urban. One platform serves the pump mechanic, the district officer (grounded in the national WASH MIS) and the utility front line — where every resolved anomaly feeds the non-revenue water dashboard.
